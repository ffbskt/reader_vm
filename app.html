Text simplifier

You have a book you'd love to read in its original language — but it's still too hard. Give the app something simple you already understand (a textbook, a graded reader, or just a word list), and it rewrites your hard book using only words you know, keeping every word you don't as a hover translation. Read it online or as a PDF, at the difficulty you choose.

📱 Prefer chat? Open the Telegram bot — send it a book, get it back simplified.

    3Level of simplification

    The level = how much of the unknown TEXT stays. The most frequent unknown words are kept until they cover that share of the unknown word occurrences — so the four levels feel evenly spaced while reading. Only these four options exist, so every translated page is cached and never paid for twice.
